WELCOME!

In a nutshell, we are a collective of educators, researchers and those interested in research in Environmental Education. Our proposal is to catalog master's dissertations and doctoral theses linked to environmental issues and educational processes in Latin America and the Caribbean. This work aims to contribute to the systematic and theoretical consolidation of the field of research in Environmental Education.
